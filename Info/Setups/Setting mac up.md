1. In Settings:
	1. Login apple id
	2. Control Centre
		1. Wifi > don't show in menu bar
		2. Battery > Show percentage
		3. Music recognition > show in control centre
		4. Keyboard brightness > show in control centre
		5. Date > flash time separator + announce time quarter hour
	3. Desktop & Dock
		1. Widgets > do not show on desktop, do not show in stage manager
		2. Windows > ask to keep changes when closing documents
		3. Windows > tiles window shouldn't have margins
	4. Display
		1. More space resolution
	5. Spotlight
		1. remove unnecessary items
	6. Sound
		1. Play feedback when volume is changed
	7. Keyboard
		1. Turn backlight off after 5min inactivity
