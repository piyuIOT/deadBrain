# Audible analysis of recordings in various scenarios:
- Male
	- Vehicle: 
		- Gasoline car do not cause any significant noise in both cases: Standing (with engine running) or Moving (in straight roads or on rough roads). 
		- Bike not yet tested
		- According to a research paper airplane could create noise in BCM (not yet tested)
	- Movement: For any movement, hardware needs to be more compact otherwise cables and connectors cause lot of noise due to significant movement
		- Most movement themself do not introduce any specific noise (but recordings contain noise due to hardware setup)
		- The BERT score reduces in activities because the subject got breathless after 2 mins of activity and couldn't properly speak
	- Food
		- Hard food items, especially eating chips cause lot of noise
	- Behaviour
		- There is some noise in scratching beard, but not significant

		- You can hear jaw clicking noise properly
		- You can properly hear snoring
