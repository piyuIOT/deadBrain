# Issues & Observations
### Piezo things
**Accel readings produced by various vibration sources** (All data at 150Hz) :  
- at 50% **MACBOOK** volume, for [150hz test tone](https://youtu.be/cZaJzjMexfM?si=-shJP2vELU_Aflqg), macbook produce roughly **10mg** accel readings perpendicular to the surface
- in normal close convo audible speech **HUMAN** produce **10mg** accel reading (ya, I was producing very close to 150, I checked)
- This **[BONE CONDUCTION TRANSDUCER](https://www.alibaba.com/product-detail/RC-BC02-sports-headphones-Small-Rectangle_1601386916131.html)** produce anywhere from **100mg - 1000mg** based on volume and pressure applied to it.
**Piezo readings in mV** :  
- Piezo does not give any output on this human voice
- Piezo gives roughly 42mV output when accel reads 450mg (same 150hz tone played via BC speaker)
- Piezo gives roughly 70mV output when accel reads 750mg (same 150hz tone played via BC speaker)
- Piezo has 0.5 - 1 mv noise when no signal is there
- Piezo has 2 - 2.5 mv 50hz noise (probably power line)

At 4000Hz:  
- On Bone conduction transducer, when accel show 40mg, piezo show 7.8mV
- On Bone conduction transducer, when accel show 20mg, piezo show 3.5mV
- On mac speaker, accel show 2mg at 50% volume, piezo shows nothing


> If we do a very rough extrapolation in both 150Hz, and 4kHz case. we would get expected piezo readings below the noise floor for piezo!
